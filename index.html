🎥 Practice Companion

This watches your body position through your camera and gives you a skeleton overlay and quantitative movement data while you practice — hang time, estimated jump height, leg-split angle, and beat timing. Nothing is ever uploaded anywhere — everything happens on this device. Your session is recorded locally so you can review it afterwards (useful for practicing alone), but the video is only kept in your browser and is discarded when you leave unless you choose to save it to your device. It's a technique-improving tool, not a judge — it only measures numbers (height, angle, timing), it never scores your artistry or technique. Height and angle are estimates from a single camera, not lab-grade motion capture, so use them to track your own progress over time rather than as an exact measurement.
